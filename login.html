This is where we will post all tee times, results and information regarding the Pacific Group Golf 2021-2022 Season. To check your tee time for the next event, go to the Tee Sheets menu and choose Tee Times. If you want to confirm what tees you will be playing from, from the Tee Times page choose the Individual tab. The number next to your name indicates your course playing handicap. We have broken the Season into Two Divsions - Those with handicaps and those without. Withing the handicap division we have three flights. Prizes will be awarded each week for the top finishers in each flight as well as those without a handicap. In addition, points will be awarded based on your finishing position in your flight. At the end of the season, those with the most points in each division will also win prizes. Each tournament will also have a blind skins contest across all flights/divisions. The low net score on each hole, except Par 3s, will win the skin. Ties will carry over to the next hole. In the event there is no winner on the 18th hole, that skin and any carry over holes will be applied to the next tournament.
